About Monitor Consults

Founded in 2021 at the height of the COVID-19 pandemic as a bold response to uncertainty

Our Story

Founded in 2021 at the height of the COVID-19 pandemic, Monitor Consults began as a bold response to uncertainty—helping organizations and businesses adapt, survive, and thrive in rapidly changing environments.

What started as a solo freelance consultancy has grown into a trusted partner for global clients seeking clarity, structure, and success in their projects.

What We Do

We help organizations and businesses turn ideas into actionable, results-driven projects. Whether you're launching a new initiative, scaling impact, or strengthening internal systems, we bring the structure, strategy, and support you need to succeed.

Scope of Operation

Headquartered in Kenya, our work spans across East Africa and beyond, with projects successfully delivered in Kenya, Uganda, Tanzania, the UK, and the USA. Whether working locally or collaborating with global teams, we bring contextual expertise and international standards to every engagement.
